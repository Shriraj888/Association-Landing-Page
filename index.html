About JJITA

The Jalgoan Jilha Information Technology Association is your premier network for IT and security professionals.

Our Mission

To create a thriving ecosystem for computer and CCTV dealers through knowledge sharing, collaboration, and mutual growth.

Our Vision

To be the driving force behind technological advancement and business success in the Jalgoan region.

What We Do

Organize events, facilitate networking, provide training, and advocate for our members' interests.

This membership provides us with a powerful voice in policy advocacy, collective bargaining strength, and access to national-level networking opportunities that benefit all our members.
